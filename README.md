## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes Uber ride data to uncover trends in bookings, revenue, trip distance, customer behavior, and vehicle performance. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze Uber ride bookings
- Monitor revenue and trip performance
- Understand customer booking patterns
- Compare vehicle performance
- Identify peak booking periods
- Create an interactive dashboard for business insights

## Key Insights

- Peak ride demand occurred during high-traffic hours.
- Online payment methods were the most preferred by customers.
- Premium vehicle categories generated higher revenue per trip.
- Most rides were successfully completed with a high completion rate.
- Business districts showed the highest pickup frequency.
